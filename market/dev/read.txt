If you would like to create an app for the v5 market; here are the requirements:

- Application title (must not exceed 18 chars)
- Application description
- A square icon for the application (1080x1080)
- An application color (matches the main color of your icon)
- Application font color (must look good on top of application color)
- Price (funds: can range from 0 to whatever)
* Download apk.zip to begin


NOTE: Misdew will deduct 30% of each sale. If your app is priced at 1.00 MDF, you will receive 0.70 MDF in Misdew Funds, and we will take the remaining 0.30 MDF.



Misdew Funds (MDF) has NOTHING to do with real money.
